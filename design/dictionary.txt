Design folder holds images and text files written to help set goals for the final product.
Materials here can be referenced during production to ensure code aligns with goals.

---

"Screen_GiftSuggestions.png" - Design of a landing screen menu that shows a list of contacts.
All contacts are displayed with a number next to them that tells the user how many active gifts have been recorded for that person.

"Screen_GiftSuggestionsList.png" - Design of a submenu screen that displays gifts recorded for a specific person chosen from the 
previous gift suggestion screen. Gifts can be added or deleted from this screen. 
Gifts that have already been bought are grayed out.

"Screen_GiftSuggestionsListGift.png" - Design of a screen that lists the details for a specific gift chosen 
from the previous gift suggestions list submenu screen. The chosen gift can be edited here.
